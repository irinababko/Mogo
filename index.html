The Auditor

reality, journalistic investigation

The hit social project, dedicated to testing the service in HoReCa, markets and other venues, where hygiene and health care are essential for the customers.

7 seasons in Ukraine (New Channel), on-going

Over 100 episodes in Russia, on-going

2 seasons in Azerbajan (ATV), on-going

The Auditor shows the underside of the service sector. What's going on in the kitchen of your favorite restaurant? What secrets are stored in the mattress and the pillow you sleep on in an expensive hotel? Will the porter or the waiter provide you with custom services for your own money? In each episode, the crew headed by the Auditor travel to different cities and walks with inspections in its hotels, cafes and restaurants, without warning the owners and staff about their visit.

The host behaves as normal, but demanding consumer and assesses each institution on six criteria: exterior, interior, pricing, cleanliness, service and features. Inspector is principled and incorruptible, so some owners act aggressively when unattractive secrets of their establishments are shown to the wide audience.

At the end of each test the Auditor informs staff and the audience of his opinion on the institutions by each criterion and proclaims his conclusion: the show The Auditor shall or shall not recommend this place across the country.

The recommendation is accompanied by a certificate from the program, which the owners of establishments proudly place on the facades of their venues.

Everything will be fine

studio

An entertaining studio show for the whole family with daily tips and themes developed for over 200 episodes.

Hit daily series in Ukraine.

Within several years an unknown host became the most published person on the magazine covers in the country with over 40M population.

The show brings together the interests of women and men on the household management, family budget, education, personal care and culinary arts.
The main difference from similar shows is in the form of presenting the information. Each episode is a set of conversations with leading experts. The studio is a kind of host’s house where her fellow experts are invited. In an entertaining way they give practical tips that are applicable in everyday life: coaches help viewers to lose weight and start dieting properly, celebrity moms share their tips on parenting, chefs give useful tips on cooking. Domestic and family problems are discussed in an interesting and unusual way, with an illustrative demonstration of how to resolve them. The tasks (transforming looks, getting rid of the stains, creating lamp shades, doing work-outs, cooking dinners, etc.) are completed within the studio with the audience involved.
The show has five main vertical blocks (rubrics) and a horizontal one (with changeable vertical headings), each has its permanent experts. The symbol of the project – a seven petal flower – is very significant, too. Each petal of it symbolizes a separate section:
Red - Psychology, Orange - Children, animals , Yellow - Cooking, Green - Health & Fitness, Blue - Finance, Dark blue - House, Violet - Beauty This TV show aims to inspire viewers for making positive changes in their lives.

Peculiarities of the nation work

reality, travel

Two comedians are travelling around the globe to experience the specifics of local professions which are considered regular in the country of origin and may appear exotic, tough or even dangerous for the foreigner.

2 seasons in Ukraine (ICTV), on-going

Some people are traveling abroad for shopping, some are interested in foreign culture and cooking, but the presenters of the travel show "Peculiarities of the national work" go to exotic countries in search of new jobs.

They will have a difficult career path in the popular professions of India, South Korea, China, Thailand and other Asian countries.

"Do you dream of working abroad? Dream carefully! "- the hosts joke. After all, the work, which in some countries is considered ordinary and simple, for our guys might be unbearable and sometimes even dangerous.

The hosts of the show approach their journeys with full responsibility. In order to debunk all the myths and show the viewer all the distinctive features of working in exotic countries, they will spare no effort mastering new professions with humor and, of course, diligence.

Lean and Mean

reality, consumer investigation

An extreme savings reality show, that shares different life-hack ideas that will come in handy in the everyday life or might shock and amaze the viewers.

2 seasons in Ukraine (New Channel), on-going

Extreme savings show. For two months hosts will hold the experiment: what you can save on considerably without compromising the traditional way of living? They will use all the known methods of saving money, as well as the experience of Ukrainian and foreign lifehackers.

How to save money on clothes and still look great? How to eat in the restaurant and not legally pay for the food? How to reduce the amount of payment for utility services? In the last episodes the hostess will show how to save on pregnancy, childbirth and infant care, as she starred in the project, while carrying a child.

Each episode is devoted to a particular topic (beauty, food, work etc.)

Love Survivors

reality

A reality show that tests young couples, who are about to get married, on their ability to co-operate and co-exist.
They will have to pass 5 challenges (including the ones that will check their fidelity, mutual respect, solicitude and trust) in order to win the cash prize.

2 seasons in Ukraine (New Channel), on-going
ready-made series became a hit in Russia, Estonia and Latvia

An emotional crash-test for lovebirds, who are planning to get married.

In every episode one couple travels to a foreign country/beautiful location to win the money for the wedding of their dreams and to see whether it is a true love that connects them or it is just an admiration or a habit.

Upon arrival the heroes will have to pass five extreme challenges, that will test their fidelity, mutual support and understanding.

For each completed test they will receive the Key. After completing all the tests the couple will face 5 Boxes. Only some of them contain money. The more keys they collect, the better their chances to win the prize are.

... if their love survives the trials by the end of the show.

daddy's house

reality

An entertaining reality show for the whole family, where the father switches places with the mother for a week and has to fulfil  number of challenges in order to win a cash prize.

6 seasons in Ukraine (STB), on-going

2 seasons in Latvia (TV3), on-going

Each episode contains a story of one family which has a certain division of responsibilities. Mommy is responsible for coziness, and taking care of everything, including cooking, cleaning, laundry, kids and, sometimes, her husband. While Daddy once being at home usually eats, relaxes, watches TV or passionately plays computer games.
For the Family to win the cash prize, Daddy has to replace Mommy, who will go for a one-week vacation and do everything she has always dreamt about, but never had time for (i.e. she may parachute out, or scuba dive… or just sleep all day long).
Daddy will stay home with children. He will spend the first day doing things, which, as he’s sure, his wife usually does – i.e. practically doing nothing. On the next day he will discover her actual schedule and the list of her daily duties. The Daddy’s challenge is to scrupulously follow the schedule by performing all parental and household duties, as well as certain tasks set by his wife without outside help.
In case Daddy uses his opportunity to call Mommy or to ask for extra help from friends or relatives, every hint will diminish the sum of the prize. If Daddy properly copes with all tasks without using the opportunity to call Mommy back ahead of time and using extra help, the family will win the cash prize.

save our family

reality

Family reality show that helps married couples on the verge of divorce to save their marriage and to improve their relations with children.
It is dedicated to divorce prevention, social adaptation of characters, psychological aid to children and building bridges between generations in families.

6 seasons in Ukraine (STB), on-going

The super-challenge of the project is not only to help certain people in reaching mutual understanding, but also to teach viewers to resolve their family problems by themselves using the example of the specific married couples.
As a first step, a husband and a wife will go to a psychologist to explain the primary cause of the rupture between them. The psychologist will make the diagnosis and determine the main aspects of treatment. To reach the goal, the couple will have to go through 4 stages of rehabilitation:
The First Stage: In order to understand each other better, they will swap their roles for one day – the Husband will go to his Wife’s work instead of her, and the Wife will go to her Husband’s work instead of him. The characters must experience themselves, the living conditions and the stresses experienced by their soul mates on regular basis.
The Second Stage: The main challenge is to build bridges, to bring back the bygone affection and love, to refresh the family way of life. One of the characters has to arrange a romantic date with his/her soul mate.
The Third Stage: To heal the “scars of the past”. The psychologist will help the couple to identify the original cause of their family problem and give advices on how to overcome this obstacle.
The Final Stage: The characters will have to overcome the stress situation by putting into practice all advices and recommendations received from the project. This challenge will show whether the marriage couple managed to resolve their problems and whether they are ready for a new happy life after the project.
